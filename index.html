Projects

Tunnel

Minimal maze puzzle

PC title during which development I mantained its design flex and simple, while paying more attention on building strong code and producing frameworks reusable in future projects. A more extensive use of GitHub helped me organizing the efforts and protecting the game.

Ploppem

Minimal endless runner

My main focus for this game was to produce something easy to understand and play, with more than an hour of content.
To achieve my objectives I developed an endless generation of enemies, and I strived to find a balance between a straightforward design and a simple aesthetic, which has been the biggest challenge to overcome for the project.
Developing various additional systems like cosmetics & different languages support, has helped me stay focused and not suffer too much from burnout.

Nodib

One-Finger puzzle game

I started this project to take a break from the development of Ploppem, and to experiment with a more puzzle-like gameplay, together with an expanded use of social medias to share my progress and gather feedback.
Due to its temporary nature I had to use shortcuts and I wasn't able to produce much content, but the result was still satisfing.

Oath

Single-player hack'n'slash

Developed during my second year on a Game Design course together with all the others school departments (Concept Art, Game Programming, 3D Art).

This experience taught me the importance of organization and schedule during a common effort.
