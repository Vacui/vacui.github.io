Ploppem

Ploppem is a minimal endless runner made for the mobile platform. I developed it as a side project to increase my portfolio as a Technical Designer.

It started out as something cartoonish and complex, but after encountering several difficulties during development, its scope has been reduced to its current state. However, I am satisfied with the result and I thanks those who supported me, especially during the most stressful moments.

KEY FEATURES
  • Endless one input game, play as much as you want.
  • Two major palettes (black and white).
  • Customize further your experience by changing the enemy colour schemes and the background
